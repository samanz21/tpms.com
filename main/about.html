ABOUT US



VISION

TPMS's vision is to provide universal access to the world's best education. They aim to create a platform that empowers people to learn without limits and overcome barriers such as geographic location, financial constraints, or lack of resources.

MISSION

TPMS's mission is to transform lives through learning. They strive to offer high-quality education and skill-building opportunities to individuals worldwide, regardless of their background or circumstances. TPMS collaborates with leading universities, organizations, and instructors to provide a diverse range of courses and programs that address the evolving needs of learners in the digital age.



TIMELINE OF TRAINING PROVIDER MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

September 2021

The idea for TPMS is conceived by a group of students in Multimedia University.

October 2021

Initial planning and research are conducted to assess the feasibility of the project.

November 2021

Development of the TPMS platform begins, including designing the user interface and backend infrastructure.

January 2022

The development team completes the initial version of TPMS, including basic course listing and enrollment features.

April 2022

The team focuses on marketing and promoting TPMS to attract students and instructors.

May 2022

Collaboration with subject matter experts and instructors begins to expand the course offerings.

July 2022

The development team introduces new features, such as discussion forums and progress tracking, to enhance the learning experience.

January 2023

TPMS expands its course catalog, offering specialized courses and degree programs in various disciplines.

February 2023

The platform integrates additional learning tools, such as interactive quizzes and assignments, to enhance student engagement.

May 2023

TPMS launches a mobile application, making the platform accessible on smartphones and tablets.

June 2023

Partnerships are established with other educational institutions and organizations to further expand the course offerings and provide collaborative learning opportunities.
